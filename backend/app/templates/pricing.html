Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as an "analysis"?

Each video session (live or recorded) counts as one analysis, regardless of duration.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade at any time. We'll prorate the difference.

Is there a free trial?

Yes, all plans include a 14-day free trial with full features.

How is my data protected?

All data is encrypted end-to-end. We never store videos without explicit consent.
